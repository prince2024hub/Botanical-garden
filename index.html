Welcome to the Botanical Garden of India

About the Garden

The Botanical Garden of India is a magnificent park that showcases the rich biodiversity of India. Explore the lush landscapes, rare species of plants, and serene surroundings. We aim to preserve the rich heritage of Indian flora while providing an educational space for visitors.

Our Garden Areas

Tropical Garden

Explore a variety of tropical plants and trees from all over the world.

Herb Garden

A collection of medicinal plants and herbs that have been used for centuries.

Succulent Garden

Marvel at the fascinating world of succulents, cacti, and desert plants.
